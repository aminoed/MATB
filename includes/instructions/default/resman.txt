<h1>Task introduction</h1>

<p>In the resource management task, participants must limit the leakage from the two main tanks (A and B) using a set of backup tanks and pumps, each with its own power.</p>

<p>The participant's task is to turn on/off the numbered pumps to bring tanks A and B as close as possible to their normal levels.</p>

<p>Some pumps can sometimes malfunction.</p>
